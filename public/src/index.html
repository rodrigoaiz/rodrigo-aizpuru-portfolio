Educational Web-resources

This is a course made for the new admission students of the CCH UNAM so they can get familiar with the college educational model and some studying strategies for their new academic starting life.

I have developed the layout design of the site and then build the HTML and CSS and co-working with another web designer and an engineer. The site has a back-end technology with PHP/Twig/JavaScritp languages.

The course is open to the public but saves the results of the exercises when a student or professor logs in. This is helpful for statistics of the new college students.
